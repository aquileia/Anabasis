Journey

Considerations

With their provisions plundered and the villages at the oases not yet restocked, the direct route back is impossible.

Nobody would make a detour to pass through mountains, quite the opposite. But we need a mountain pass for "Living rock"
The central plains only have two adjacent mountainous regions: The Dulatus hills and the Gryphon mountain.

Track 1: South to the Sleepless Sea

Weldyn – just east of the Dulatus hills – through Dulatus hills – Fort Than – west towards the Aethenwood, but rejected – city on the coast of the inner sea – sea travel

Advantages:
  • Less restrictions as the area isn't shown in mainline
Main problems:
  • Need a port (khalifate traders?) on area mapped by TSG
  • There's only the mountains of peril between them and their destination, why wait for ships?

Track 2: North to Elensefar

Weldyn – Tath – dwarven caves north of gryphon mountain – hills north of gr. Mountain / valley of death – grey woods – Halstead – Elensefar – sea travel

Advantages:
  • Cross references HttT: Huge falcons! Exciting! – valley of death
  • Cross reference Liberty: impenetrable fortress Halstead
  • Elensefar can muster a sufficient fleet
Main problems:
  • In case we choose the valley of death, why is Konrad still batling off the undead there in HttT? Didn't we get their mastermind?
  • Can we have trolls and dwarves in the hills south of gryphon mountain?
This shall not be the end: Battles await you back home!
